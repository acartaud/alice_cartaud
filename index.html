Research Interests


Key Words: space perception, interpersonal distance, physiological response, emotional facial expressions, peripersonal space, threat perception, emotional context


I am interested in the spatial coding of threat in a social context. More precisely, I have studied to what extent the adjustment of interpersonal distance is related to the physiological response triggered by individuals within the peripersonal space. I am also interested in how emotional information from the context can affect interpersonal distance with neutral conspecifics.


I'll defend my PhD thesis on January 19th, 2021

Title: Interrelation between the sensorimotor and emotional components of social space: behavioral and psychophysiological evidence
